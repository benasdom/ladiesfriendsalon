About us




Here at LF'S Effects, we provide the best of services in cosmetics and treatments. We offer services such as hair dressing,facial treatments,barbering,body massage,pedicure, manicure,hair treatments,and so much more! LF's Effects is a sole-proprietal buisiness, that began in the year 2000. It originally existed as Ladies Friend Designer Salon. The enterprise is under the GHABA organisation(ASSI), at Adenta, which is also affiliated with the industrial and commercial workers union. Lf's Effects is owned by Gladys Asumadu also the president of GHABA association. She is a T.Vet facilitator, NVTI trade test, cosmetologist/beautician.
